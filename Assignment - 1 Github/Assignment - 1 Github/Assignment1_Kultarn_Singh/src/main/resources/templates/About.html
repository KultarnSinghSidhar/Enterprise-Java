Our Mission

Anime Event 2025 is committed to bringing fans the best of anime, manga, and Japanese pop culture. We provide a platform where artists, cosplayers, voice actors, and industry experts connect directly with fans, fostering a vibrant community.

Our History

Since 2010, Anime Event has grown from a 500-person meetup to a 60,000+ attendee celebration. Our past guests include top Japanese voice actors, award-winning cosplayers, and exclusive sneak peeks of upcoming anime seasons.

Highlights from Previous Years

  • Live performance by JAM Project (2022)
  • Premiere screening of My Hero Academia Season 6 (2023)
  • World cosplay championship qualifier (2024)
