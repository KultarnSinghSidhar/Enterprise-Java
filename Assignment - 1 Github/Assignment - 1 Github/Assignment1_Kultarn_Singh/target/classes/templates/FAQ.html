Cosplay & Props

Are weapons or props allowed?

Props must pass safety inspection. No real or functional weapons are permitted.

Can I change costumes on-site?

Yes, we have dedicated cosplay changing rooms and makeup stations.

Can I enter the cosplay contest?

Yes, sign-ups are open until noon on Saturday at the Cosplay Desk near the main entrance.

Are group cosplays allowed?

Absolutely! Just make sure the entire group registers together for the contest.

Food & Drinks

Can I bring outside food?

Small snacks and water bottles are okay, but full meals or alcohol are not allowed.

Are there food vendors onsite?

Yes! We have a food court with Japanese street food, ramen, boba tea, and vegan options.

Is there a water refill station?

Lost & Found

I lost something at the event. What do I do?

Visit the Lost & Found booth near the registration area or email lost@animeevent2025.com after the event.

What if I lose my badge?

Go to the Badge Help Desk. A reprint may cost a $10 fee with valid ID.

Accessibility & Safety

Is the venue wheelchair accessible?

Yes, all halls are fully accessible. Contact us in advance for specific accommodations.

What should I do in case of emergency?

Locate the nearest staff member or security guard. Emergency exits are clearly marked throughout the venue.

Are service animals allowed?

Yes, service animals are permitted. Please ensure they are properly identified.
